Earned certification to begin biotechnology work through the Contra Costa County (CCC) ROP Biotechnology course, developing laboratory techniques and scientific concepts used in bioscience.

This course provides students with the opportunity to develop laboratory techniques and scientific concepts used in the field of bioscience. Students enhance their problem-solving skills as they design and conduct experiments and develop critical thinking skills through research and discussion about issues related to advances in biotechnology.

**Skills gained:** micropipetting, gel electrophoresis, centrifugation, PCR, DNA extraction and analysis, culturing bacterial strains, and more.

**Lab topics:** extraction and analysis of DNA, electrophoresis, bacteriological techniques, culturing strains of bacteria, plasmid transformation, and more.
