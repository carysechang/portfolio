Medical school reviewers often look for evidence of leadership, initiative, and service. This entry can capture those experiences even when they are not explicitly clinical.

Good examples include:

- mentoring younger students
- organizing service projects
- coordinating a club or campus initiative
- supporting peer wellness or academic programming

Use the description to explain what you contributed and how the role helped you grow as a teammate, communicator, and future physician.
