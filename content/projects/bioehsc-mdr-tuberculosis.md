A 7-week competition hosted by the UC Berkeley BioEngineering Society that challenges competitors to develop a novel solution to a disease, create a research abstract, present a poster to industry academics, and pitch the product to industry investors.

**Approach:** Synthesizing an anti-VEGF antibody to disable the supply of oxygen and nutrients delivered to granulomas via blood that sustains tuberculosis bacteria in multi-drug resistant tuberculosis.
